Bang! Bang!

Product design, UI/UX

Designing an experience that overcomes the barriers and ideals of what it is like to play tennis.

What we did

THE ISSUE

Tennis has always had barriers when it comes to participating or even starting it, examples of these barriers are the sport is too expensive, lack of partners, and being more serious than it is fun and many more.

Our approach

UNIQUE

What does it do?

Bang! Bang! emits sounds, selected by the user through the app, randomly every time the user hits the tennis ball with the racquet thanks to the sensor implemented into the smart object.

Smart Device

BRINGING IT
TO LIFE

We wanted to create a working prototype that would provide a better understanding of how the system is developed for users. It also offered quick user feedbacks that lead to better solutions for the smart device.

Low-fidelity

THE START

Breaking up the app into three simple sections; Home, Store and Statistic. We decided that the Apple Watch app was a great way to start off the foundation of our compainion app where everything was accesible within an instant of connection the smart device.

High-fidelity

A VISUAL
WALKTHROUGH

Users are able to connect to their smart device instantly when opening the app. From there, they are greated with three screens: Home, where all information is accesible to control the smart device is located, Store, users can browse through the sound store and download their own personal sounds to play with, Statistic, a tab where users can go in and see how often they have played tennis which will give them motivation to play more.

Connecting

When the app opens, users are right away asked to connect their smart device to the application by capturing a photo of the QR code on the smart device.

Home

Once connected, users will have access to everything they would need to start using their smart device on the Home page. From controling the volume to making a playlist, users can right away start playing.

Store

The store is based off of the Apple's App Store to give a familiarity usage when navigating through the store. Users have a variety of themes that they will have easy access to when on the main page of the Store, such as Trending, Sales, and Suggestions that are based off of your past purchases.

The Bang! Bang! store also based off of themes throughout the year such. For example, if it is near Christmas, users will see sounds based of what holiday or event that is going on in the duration of when browsing the store.
